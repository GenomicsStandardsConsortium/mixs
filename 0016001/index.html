    Extension: built environment (BuiltEnvironment)

    A collection of terms appropriate when collecting samples and sequencing samples obtained in the built-up environment, which includes terms for surface material, humidity, temperature, moisture and occupancy type along with specific metadata terms describing the indoor air, building and sample properties.
