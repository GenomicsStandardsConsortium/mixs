Generated by the [diff-summary skill](../../diff-summary-skill/README.md) from
`schema_comparison_results.yaml` in this directory. This is the format-crossing
transition from the last Excel MIxS (v5, 600 terms) to the first LinkML MIxS
(v6.0.0, 804 terms).

**Added:** 226 terms (only in v6.0.0). **Removed:** 18 terms (only in v5).
**Shared:** 573.

**Renamed (6):** `16s_recover` to `x_16s_recover`, `16s_recover_software` to
`x_16s_recover_software`, `health_disease_stat` to `host_disease_stat`,
`votu_class_appr` to `otu_class_appr`, `votu_db` to `otu_db`,
`votu_seq_comp_appr` to `otu_seq_comp_appr`. (The `16s` terms gain an `x_`
prefix because LinkML names cannot start with a digit; `votu` became `otu`.)

**Removed for structural reasons (3):** `env_package` (package membership now
handled by the class hierarchy), `investigation_type` (now implicit in checklist
selection), `submitted_to_insdc` (INSDC submission status no longer tracked).

**Format-migration changes (not semantic):** almost every shared term shows a
change in `value_syntax` (579), `occurrence` (578), and `expected_value` (576).
These are not content edits. They reflect how the v5 Excel columns map onto
LinkML: the free-text "value syntax", "occurrence", and "expected value" columns
became structured LinkML slots (patterns, cardinality, ranges). `preferred_unit`
changed for 214 terms in the same way.

**Real wording changes:** 54 terms have an actual `definition` change; 34 have an
`example` change. These are the edits worth reading term by term.

**Notes:** this diff is dominated by the Excel-to-LinkML migration itself, so the
raw count of "579 changed terms" overstates semantic change. The substantive
signal is the 226 added terms, 18 removed, 6 renames, 3 structural removals, and
the 54 real definition edits. Because both endpoints are frozen releases, this
result never changes; it is kept as a historical record, not regenerated at
release time.
